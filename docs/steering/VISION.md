## Product Purpose

At house parties, music control is a social problem: one person owns the aux and everyone else is at their mercy. Fissa solves this by turning the queue into a shared space. Guests join a party session via PIN and collectively shape what plays next through track additions and voting — while the host retains ultimate authority over the room.

## Target Users

| Actor       | Context                                                  | Primary goals                                                          |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Party Host  | Hosting a gathering with a Spotify Premium account       | Start a fissa, control the vibe, remove tracks or guests when needed   |
| Party Guest | Attending a party, joining via shared PIN                | Add favourite tracks, vote on the queue, influence what plays          |

## Core Principles

1. **Social first** — Every feature must work better with a crowd than alone. If it makes no difference whether one person or ten are in the room, reconsider it.
2. **Frictionless joining** — Guests should be in the party within seconds. No account required to join; barriers favour the host, not the crowd.
3. **Spotify-native** — Lean on Spotify's ecosystem for discovery, playback, and account management. Do not rebuild what Spotify already does well.
4. **Host accountability** — The host sets the tone. They always hold override power — over tracks, members, and playback — and bear responsibility for the experience.

## Strategic Goals

- [ ] Fissa is the go-to aux app at real house parties — measured by sessions with 5+ guests adding tracks and voting.

| Queue        | Track additions, vote tallying, playback order                  | Track, Vote                     |
| Playback     | Spotify API integration, real-time sync, skip/pause controls    | SpotifyService, Queue state     |
| Gamification | Rewarding participation across sessions                         | Badge, BadgeProgress            |

## Out of Scope

- Non-Spotify streaming platforms (Apple Music, YouTube, etc.)
- Persistent playlists — fissa sessions are ephemeral; no saving or replaying past queues
- Social network features — no following, friend lists, or user profiles beyond the active party context
